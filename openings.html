Open Positions

Open positions are listed below. If you are interested in one or more positions please send an email to info@organeyez.co with your contact information, resume, and a brief explanation of why you want to join OrganEyez.

After you email us, we will contact you to explain more about our process.

All workers are expected to work an average of anywhere between 1 and 10 hours a week. Your hours are very flexible and we won't question your time commitment unless you just disappear!

We are looking for people with these qualities:

  • passionate about social justice
  • organized and able to work independently without being micromanaged
  • intrinsically motivated
  • good at communicating ideas, feelings, and thought processes
  • comfortable with giving and receiving both positive and negative feedback
  • A knack for producing excellent work
  • Access to a laptop and able to participate in remote video-call meetings

Open Positions

  • Security Software Engineer

  • Social Media Coordinator

  • Facebook Community Moderator

  • Community Relations & Partnerships Coordinator
    • Develops relationships with activists and organizations that are users or potential users of our technologies and/or can partner with us on projects
    • Helps to increase awareness of OrganEyez within activist communities
