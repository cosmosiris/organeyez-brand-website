Strategic Planning Templates

Strategic planning is a comprehensive process that involves many steps from mapping out where you are and where you want to go to creating your strategic plan, and adjusting it over time. Below are some helpful resources for every stage.

These templates may not fit your exact situation but they can be molded and adjusted to your circumstance or provide ideas that you can use to create your own templates. Take a look at these worksheets and guides to see if you find one or more that work for you or your organization.

  • Action Map by Washington Nonprofits
  • Sample timeline/workplan for strategic planning by Washington Nonprofits
  • A step by step guide to strategic planning in non-profits by Washington Nonprofits
  • Nonprofit Strategic Plan template by smartsheet.com
  • Mission Statement Assessment Tool by Washington Nonprofits
  • Strategic Vision template by smartsheet.com
  • Strategic Planning Workbook: a course by Steve Cohen & Bill Eimicke
  • Human Resources Strategic Plan by smartsheet.com
  • Social Media Marketing Planning Guide by simplymeasured.com is very comprehensive and includes worksheets
  • Generic Strategic Plan by smartsheet.com can be applied to many different use cases
