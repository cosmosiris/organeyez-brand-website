While volunteering as a community organizer I noticed that many of the social justice organizations I worked with were struggling to balance responsibilities, grow membership, strengthen relationships, deepen knowledge, and hold members accountable--all of which are necessary to building a sustainable and efficient organization. Little accountability, lack of transparency, and slow decision-making also kept really important projects from getting off the ground. Organ Eyez is my effort to use my growing software engineering skills to give organizers a better shot at changing their communities and ultimately, changing the world.
