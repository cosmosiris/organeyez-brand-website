Organeyez Podcast

Follow our podcast blog here

The goal of our podcast is to seek out activists who are using valuable strategies to improve the growth and development of their organizations or the movements they are involved in.

We've identified 4 different types of activists and aim to interview folks who have

  • had significant impact on legislature
  • had significant impact on a community
  • taken a creative approach to addressing social justice problems

If you run an organization or participate in grassroots movements, this podcast is for you.
