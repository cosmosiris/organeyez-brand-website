How Can You Support?

Financial

Though we are an LLC, we provide all of our services free of charge and intend to keep things this way. We believe that useful technology, resources, and information should be accessible to community organizations who are positively impacting the world.

Nonetheless, we still have costs. We are looking to becoming a community funded company that is able to cover its expenses and compensation costs purely from monthly supporter payments made to our Patreon account. We believe that by sending monthly payments to OrganEyez of $10/month or more, we can become the largest community funded mission-oriented corporation in the United States.

Whether you are a small donor or a large donor, we ask that you commit to monthly payments rather than 1 time lump sum donations. Not only does this help us to distribute compensation fairly but it also helps to make YOU a long-term investor in our long-term project. To learn more about our expenses and compensation structure, please visit the about page.

Energetic

If you cannot support financially, there are many other ways. If you are an activist we ask you to share our podcast with your organizing community, join our online community, and use our applications. If you are not an activist you can support by promoting awareness of who we are and what we do!

If you have skills you would like to contribute to our organization, feel free to take a look at our open positions and reach out to us about joining our team!
