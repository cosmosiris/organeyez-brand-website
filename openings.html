Open Positions

Open positions are listed below. If you are interested in one or more positions please send an email to info@organeyez.co with your contact information, resume, and a brief explanation of why you want to join Organeyez.

After you email us, we will contact you to explain more about our process.

All workers are expected to work an average of anywhere between 1 and 10 hours a week. Your hours are very flexible and we won't question your time commitment unless you just disappear!

We are looking for people with these qualities:

  • passionate about social justice
  • organized and able to work independently without being micromanaged
  • intrinsically motivated
  • good at communicating ideas, feelings, and thought processes
  • comfortable with giving and receiving both positive and negative feedback
  • A knack for producing excellent work
  • We are a distrbuted team so access to a laptop and ability to participate in remote video-call meetings is crucial

Open Positions

Engineering

  • Software Engineer
    • Checks applications for security vulnerabilities and fixes them
    • Meets with dev team to discuss new features and best strategies
    • Helps dev team make technical decisions
    • Helps design and create new applications

Media & Communications

  • Creative Content Coordinator
    • Organizes video, meme, and promotional content for social media
    • Schedules content to be distributed on social media platforms.
    • Collaborates with graphic designer and filmmaker
  • Newsletter Coordinator
    • Creates the weekly newsletter and distributes it once a week

Operations

  • Administrative Assistant
    • Manages email communications
    • Organizes meetings and the yearly retreat
    • Directly assists the Executive Director with many different types of administrative tasks

Community & Partnerships

  • Online Community Moderator
    • Maintains the Organeyez Online Community (currently on Facebook) by interacting with members, answering questions, monitoring activities and conversations, resolving conflicts, and posting content.
  • Community Relations & Partnerships Coordinator
    • Develops relationships with activists and organizations to help build our following and user base.
    • Helps to increase awareness of Organeyez within activist communities
    • This is a very social role that involves attending events and getting involved in local social justice activities
