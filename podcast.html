OrganEyez Podcast

The state of our society makes grassroots organizing very difficult when this kind of work is exactly what is needed in order to change the state of our society. It's a huge catch 22 and despite all of the passion organizers have, it is very difficult to build and maintain successful organizations.

The goal of our podcast is to seek out activists who have found valuable strategies for improving the growth and development of their organizations and the movements they facilitate.

Once we find these activists, we reach out and ask them to be a guest on our podcast so that we can get into the depths of their stories and the details of their strategies.

Hosts Iris Nevins and Bertisha Combs from the OrganEyez team, have ample experience doing community organizing work in South Florida and have seen first hand how organizations struggle to stay secure, consistent, organized, and impactful while resources are thin.

If you run an organization or participate in grassroots movements, this podcast is for you.
