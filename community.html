Organeyez Online Community

Launch Date is TBD!

Join early here

The Organeyez crew plans to build an online community of movers and shakers who are really interested in learning about how activists can radically improve their outcomes. What better way to learn than in community with others who share the same passions and interests?

We have started this online community using a facebook group and plan to launch in 2019. We hope to see conversations about security, organization, growth, accountability, and much more. This community is a place to share resources, ask questions, get feedback on ideas, share thoughts, and generally just connect with others who really want to impact the world!
